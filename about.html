Objective

Our objective is to provide customers with furniture and other decor items on the move. Many people tend to move from one place to other frequently, or even like to change their surroundings according to their choice. What if the new models are no suited?



Aim

We are here to answer these questions. Consumers can rent new decor which suits their lifestyle or homeplace, and lend their currently owned decor to others, who might prefer to have them. This will ensure consumer satisfaction, they can change anytime they want, and would easily fit in budget.

For users

  • Consumers will be able to rent their choice of decor from a list of items, and keep them for the duration they decide.

  • Users can lend their decor to others if they don't want to keep it with them.

  • The services will be inter-city services for easy communication and transportation.

  • This will give many people an economic advantage, and also their choice of surroundings among themselves.
